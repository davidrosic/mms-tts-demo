This page presents the results of audio training experiments for various voices in English and Serbian. Each section compares audio samples across different training epochs and configurations (Long, Medium, Short with Prefill ON/OFF). The goal is to demonstrate improvements in voice synthesis quality over training iterations.

When listening to the files, pay attention to: (1) Clarity and naturalness of speech, (2) Similarity to the original sample, (3) Artifacts or distortions in early epochs vs. later ones, (4) Differences between Prefill ON and OFF modes, which affect processing efficiency and quality.
